Traversen

Traversen is the studentbar owned by Studentföreningen Campus Skellefteå and managed by the subsection Sexmästeriet. You can read more about SFCS and Sexmästeriet at www.studentforeningen.se!

We only take cash at Traversen so don't forget to withdraw some in advance.

To enter Traversen, you will need to have a membership at SFCS (Tip for LTU students: Teknologkåren membership includes SFCS membership).

As a member of SFCS, you are allowed to enter Traversen (for free) and also allowed to guest in ONE person that is not a member of SFCS at the door of Traversen. The guest needs to pay 20kr at the door of Traversen.

If you want to guest in more than one person, you will need to give us a guestlist through our mail traversen@traversen.se! The guestlist should include "Name Surname YYYY-MM-DD" so we can identify them at the door of Traversen. The guests still pays 20kr each at the door.

Are you a student at Campus Skellefteå and would like to work for us? Then contact us on the Campus Discord or through our mail traversen@traversen.se!

E-mail:

Wan't to have a gig at Traversen or do some business with us? The best way to reach us is by sending an e-mail to traversen@traversen.se!
